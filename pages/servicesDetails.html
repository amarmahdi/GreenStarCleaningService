Floor Cleaning

Call Greenstar Cleaning Service Ltd for immaculate floors. We offer intensive floor stripping and waxing services to leave your commercial or residential floors free of accumulated debris, grime, water and corrosion stains. From marble floors to composite vinyl tiles and hardwood floors, we undertake all floor restoration jobs.

Our state of the art floor cleaning process involves cleaning the floor to rid the stains and dirt, followed by waxing with our fine quality floor wax. We use our scrubbing machine to remove excess wax, before finally buffing out any traces of dust and dirt, leaving you with floors that shine like new.

We undertake all glass cleaning jobs in the Edmonton area, including storefront windows, multi-story building façade glass as well as residential complex windows. Volume window cleaning is best outsourced to the experts who have the patience, knowledge, and hands-on experience to undertake this task.
